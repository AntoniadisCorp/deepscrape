🚨 Danger zone

Deleting your account will:

  1. 1. Permanently delete your profile, along with your authentication associations.
  2. 2. Permanently delete all your content, including your tasks, task completions, analytics graphs, scrape data, storage, machines etc.
  3. 3. Allow your username to become available to anyone.

Important: deleting your account is unrecoverable and cannot be undone. Feel free to contact support@deepscrape.dev with any questions.
